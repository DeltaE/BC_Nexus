Hour grouping merges consecutive hours into one bracket: grouping 4 gives 6 brackets per representative day; grouping 1 keeps hourly detail.

Clusters are the representative day types the year is compressed into (e.g. winter peak, summer average). More clusters capture more seasonal and weather variety.
